About Us

We Built This for Dentists. Only Dentists.

Aug8 Agency is an America-first dental automation agency dedicated entirely to helping dental practices grow faster, smarter, and more profitably than they ever thought possible.

Our Story

Born From a Simple Frustration

We watched too many talented dentists struggle with marketing agencies that didn't understand their world — agencies that applied cookie-cutter strategies meant for restaurants or retail stores to dental practices.

The dental industry is unique. Patients have anxieties. Insurance is complex. Location is everything. And speed of follow-up is the single most important factor in converting a lead into a booked patient.

So we built a system from the ground up, specifically for dental practices. We combined professional website design, targeted Meta advertising, and our proprietary 5-minute lead alert technology into one complete, done-for-you growth engine.

Today, Aug8 Agency works with dental practices across all 50 states, and our mission remains the same: help every dentist we work with become the most trusted, most booked practice in their area.

Our Mission

Make Every Dental Practice Impossible to Ignore

We exist to give independent dental practices the same marketing firepower that the big corporate chains have — and then some. We believe every great dentist deserves a full schedule, growing revenue, and a practice that runs on a predictable patient pipeline.

We don't work with every industry. We don't split our focus. Every strategy we've developed, every tool we've built, every optimization we run is designed specifically for one thing: getting more patients into dental chairs across America.
